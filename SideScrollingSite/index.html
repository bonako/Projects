Welcome to SideLegends

This website is dedicated to bringing you key information collection of side-scrolling games for the Android system and help you save time when you are browsing through the application store to look for games. SideLegends has the information you need. Each post will include:

  • screenshots
  • reviews
  • features
A side-scrolling game or side-scroller is a video game in which the gameplay action is viewed from a side-view camera angle, and as the player's character moves left or right, the screen scrolls with them. Source: Wikipedia
