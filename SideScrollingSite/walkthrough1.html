Sword of Xolan Act 1 Level 2

  1. Collect the blue potion on the left.
  2. Kill the zombie then drop down and destroy the vase.
  3. Save the prisoner.
  4. Continue left, kill the hanging spider and the zombie, then continue up.
  5. Kill the enemy and the giant, save the prisoner.
  6. Head right and up.
  7. Escape the enemy's balls of fire by standing on the edge of the grass, then kill the enemy with your balls of fire.
  8. Continue right and kill the giant.
  9. Save the prisoner in the secret area to the right, then drop down.
  10. Kill the hanging spider and the bee with your sword.
  11. Jump only after the green slime passes you, then attack with your ball of fire.
  12. Continue right, kill the zombie, and complete the level.
